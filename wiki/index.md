## Algorithms

- [[algorithms/pbft]] ‚Äî PBFT-family (Castro‚ÄìLiskov, HotStuff, Tendermint): three-phase commit under partial synchrony; `3f+1` replicas; view change as liveness recovery.
- [[algorithms/pos]] ‚Äî PoS-finality (Casper FFG, Gasper): BFT finality gadget over a chain; two-round justify‚Üífinalise at epoch granularity; stake-weighted `3f+1` with slashing-based accountable safety.
- [[algorithms/avalanche]] ‚Äî Avalanche family (Slush ‚Üí Snowflake ‚Üí Snowball ‚Üí DAG-Avalanche; production: Snowman): BFT via repeated random `k`-peer subsampling; probabilistic finality `1 ‚àí Œµ` with `Œµ < (1 ‚àí Œ±_c/K)^Œ≤`; per-validator cost `O(K¬∑Œ≤)` independent of `n`.
- [[algorithms/dag-based]] ‚Äî DAG-based BFT (Narwhal+Tusk, Bullshark, Mysticeti): decouple data availability (DAG mempool) from ordering (anchor commit over DAG); `3f+1` threshold, async-safe, `O(n)` per-block messages, trades messages for per-node storage.
