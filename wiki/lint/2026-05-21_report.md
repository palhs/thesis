- `[[algorithms/casper-ffg]]`
- `[[algorithms/snowman]]`

production protocol:

the wiki does not use. Either (a) repoint each link to the corresponding

named protocol into its own page. Option (a) is the lower-disruption fix
and matches the current index taxonomy under `## Algorithms`.

Suggested resolution: short Researcher task in Backlog to repoint the
three links in `wiki/concepts/reproducibility.md`; no other page touched.
