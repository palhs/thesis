- **Sweep-harness performance & orchestration improvements** (introduced 2026-06-11 during T46; the full 240-run delay sweep took ~2.4 h sequential, dominated by Snowman n=25 at ~4 min/run × 8 M events/run, forcing an ad-hoc parallelization). Priority: H.** The sweep writes its CSV only after *all* runs finish, so a kill or crash at minute 80 loses everything (this is why the T46 Snowman n=25 jobs could not be safely interrupted).   - **(b) Native parallelism (`--jobs N`).
